## boule
72.5% hydration, 20% starter, 10% salt
- [ ] mix starter and warm water before adding flour and salt
- [ ] mix together until shaggy dough forms, rest for 30 minutes
- [ ] 4 sets of stretch and folds every 30 minutes
- [ ] bulk ferment (room temp) for 2.5-4 hours
- [ ] pre-shape on counter and rest for 20 minutes
- [ ] shape and place in proofing basket, cold retard 12-48 hours in fridge
- [ ] preheat dutch oven @ 450°F for 1 hour
- [ ] dough on parchment paper, score, and transfer
- [ ] 25 minutes covered, 25 minutes uncovered
## sandwich loaf
65% hydration, 10% starter, 10% salt
